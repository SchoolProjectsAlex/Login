The Umbrella Corporation is a multinational conglomerate with subsidiaries active in a variety of industries beginning in the 1980s. Umbrella has influence in the production and sale mainly of (1) cosmetics, (2) pharmaceuticals, (3) health foods, but also in chemicals, industrial machine production, consumer products, the transportation industry and tourism. Umbrella's large array of subsidiaries was typical for large-scale corporations, though it was purposely built to cover up illegal activities.
